Graduate Capstone Research Proposal

What factors are contributing to the global rise of antisemitism since the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, and what can we do about it?

3 reasons why I chose this topic:

  1. Antisemitism has been around for over two millennia, affecting Jewish communities across the world.
    • My own family was persecuted during the Holocaust.
    • A brief timeline of antisemitsm

  2. The Covid-19 pandemic has brought a spike in antisemitic incidents.
    • In 2020, an Oakland synaguage was vandalized.


  3. Despite record-breaking levels of antisemitism, most of the world remains silent on the issue.
    • Around the world, news outlets, governments, and citizens aren't speaking out.
