Global Rise in Antisemitsm

What factors are contributing to the global rise of antisemitism since the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, and what can we do about it?

Summary Info:

  • Antisemitism has been around for over two millennia, effecting Jewish communities across the world.
  • The Covid-19 pandemic has brought a spike in antisemetic incidents.
  • Despite record-breaking levels of antisemitism, most of the world remains silet on the issue.
